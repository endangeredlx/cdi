Mission Statement
The Crew Development Institute builds sustainable entertainment infrastructure by training local technicians to deliver world-class live productions. We connect diaspora expertise with local talent to create lasting workforce development in underserved markets.
Boilerplate
Crew Development Institute (CDI) is a professional training organization dedicated to developing Africa's next generation of live event production professionals. Through hands-on curriculum in lighting, audio, video, and stage management, CDI equips local technicians with the skills to deliver world-class productions.
