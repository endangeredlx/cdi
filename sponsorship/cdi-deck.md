# CDI
## Crew Development Institute
### Building Africa's Live Entertainment Workforce

---

# The Opportunity

**When global artists perform across Africa, crews are flown in from abroad. Local technicians watch from the wings.**

CDI exists to change that equation.

- **85%** of African live events rely on imported technical crews
- **$2B+** annual production spending leaves the continent
- **$5B+** projected African music industry value by 2026
- **0** formal production training programs in most West African markets

---

# What CDI Does

A comprehensive ecosystem for technical production training

## Learning Arm
School programs and hands-on training for local technicians in lighting, audio, video, and rigging. University partnerships across three continents.

## Technical Advisory
On-site and virtual production management. Industry professionals mentoring and supporting local crews on real productions.

## Production Arm
Direct production support during transition, phasing out as local teams become self-sufficient. Building capacity for world-class events.

---

# Training Tracks

## Audio
*"Where science meets vibe"*
FOH/Monitor engineering, system design, RF coordination, mixing for live performance

## Lighting
*"Paint with light, not brushes"*
GrandMA3 programming, rigging, show design, fixture knowledge, touring protocols

## Video
*"The screens everyone's watching"*
LED wall systems, camera operation, Resolume, content management, switching

## Rigging
*"Safety first, magic second"*
Load calculations, safety protocols, truss systems, load-in/out operations
