<!-- SLIDE 4: OUR APPROACH - Three pillars, visual treatment -->
# How We Build the Future

![Split-screen layout with crew-teamwork.jpg on left side]

CDI operates through three interconnected programs, each designed to create sustainable change:

**LEARNING**
School-based programs and hands-on training for local technicians. University partnerships across three continents bring academic rigor to real-world skills.

**ADVISORY**
Industry professionals working alongside local crews on actual productions. Not just teaching—mentoring through live experience.

**PRODUCTION**
Direct production support during the transition period, gradually stepping back as local teams gain confidence and capability.

## Design Notes for PDF Generation

### Visual Treatment
- Full-bleed imagery throughout
- Dark backgrounds with high-contrast typography
- Accent color: Deep blue (#1a1a2e) with gold highlights (#d4af37)
- Clean sans-serif typography (recommend: Montserrat headers, Open Sans body)

### Image Placement Guide

| Slide | Image File | Treatment |
|-------|-----------|-----------|
| Cover | stage-lights.jpg | Full-bleed, light text overlay |
| The Moment | stadium-setup.jpg | Full-bleed with vignette |
| Numbers | stage-lights.jpg | 40% opacity background |
| Approach | crew-teamwork.jpg | Left 50% split |
| Technical Arts | 4-image strip | Horizontal panels |
| The Work | crew-teamwork.jpg | Full-bleed feature |
| Leadership | Individual portraits | Grid/sequence layout |
| Impact | audio-working.jpg | Background with overlay |
| Vision | stage-lights.jpg | Full-bleed aspiration |
| CTA | stadium-setup.jpg | Gradient overlay |
| Close | truss-rig.jpg | Full-bleed dramatic |

### Typography Hierarchy
- H1: 72pt bold, white or gold
- H2: 36pt semibold
- Body: 18pt regular
- Captions: 14pt italic

### Transitions
Recommend subtle fade transitions between slides for professional presentation flow.
