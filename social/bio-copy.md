### LinkedIn Company Description (2000 characters max)
```
Building Crews Who Build Shows

The Crew Development Institute (CDI) is a professional training organization dedicated to developing Africa's next generation of live event production professionals. Through hands-on curriculum in lighting, audio, video, and stage management, CDI equips local technicians with the skills to deliver world-class productions.

OUR MISSION
We build sustainable entertainment infrastructure by training local technicians to deliver world-class live productions. We connect diaspora expertise with local talent to create lasting workforce development in underserved markets.

OUR APPROACH
Diaspora-Driven · Local Crews · Global Standards

TRAINING PROGRAMS
• Tier 1: Stagehand Fundamentals — Free 2-day program training 100+ stagehands per quarter in load-in/load-out, stage etiquette, power distribution, cable handling, and safety protocols.

• Tier 2: Specialized Tracks — 3-day intensive programs in Audio (FOH/monitor mixing, system design, RF coordination), Lighting (GrandMA3 programming, fixture rigging, show design), Video (LED wall systems, camera operations, Resolume), and Rigging (safety, load-in logistics, operational awareness).

THE MODEL
CDI deploys quarterly to Accra, Ghana with 6 senior U.S.-based production professionals. Each deployment culminates in a real live production crewed entirely by trainees under professional supervision.

LEADERSHIP
Founded by Erica D. Hayes (Lighting Designer/Educator, IATSE 728, CBU Professor), Keith Bernard (Lighting Designer/Visual Director), and Blake Brady (Producer/Operations Leader) — with combined 40+ years of concert touring, TV, and film experience.

In partnership with LiveX Africa, Accra, Ghana.

🌐 cdi-global.org
📧 partnerships@cdi-global.org
```
