## Content Templates

### Carousel Post (Instagram)
- Slide 1: Bold statement/question
- Slide 2-4: Key points/information
- Slide 5: CTA (Follow, Learn More, Apply)

### LinkedIn Long-Form
- Hook (first line grabs attention)
- Problem or context
- Solution or insight
- Call to action
- Relevant hashtags (3-5)

### TikTok/Reel
- Hook in first 2 seconds
- Quick cuts, movement
- Text overlays
- Trending sounds when appropriate
- 15-60 seconds max

### YouTube
- Strong thumbnail
- Hook in first 10 seconds
- Chapters for longer content
- End screen with subscribe CTA
