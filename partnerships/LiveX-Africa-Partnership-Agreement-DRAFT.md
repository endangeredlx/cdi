## PURPOSE

This agreement establishes LiveX Africa as CDI's **Strategic Operations Partner** in Ghana, responsible for local operations, facilities, and production support for CDI's quarterly training deployments.

---

## CDI DELIVERS

1. **Curriculum & Training**
   - Full production training curriculum (Lighting, Audio, Video, Rigging, Stagehand Fundamentals)
   - CDI retains full ownership of all curriculum materials
   - 6 senior U.S.-based production professionals per deployment

2. **Certification Program**
   - Co-branded CDI × LiveX Africa certificates for graduates
   - Crew registry access for certified technicians

3. **International Network**
   - Connections to U.S. production industry
   - Equipment manufacturer relationships
   - Grant and sponsorship opportunities

4. **Brand & Marketing**
   - Joint marketing and press coverage
   - Social media cross-promotion
   - Documentary/media content creation

---

## LIVEX AFRICA PROVIDES

1. **Facility & Equipment**
   - Training venue/facility in Accra
   - Equipment storage (secure, climate-controlled)
   - Insurance coverage for stored CDI equipment
   - Access to LiveX production gear for training purposes

2. **Local Operations**
   - Program director and local coordinator
   - Transportation coordination (airport transfers, daily transport)
   - Trainee recruitment and enrollment administration
   - Catering coordination for training sessions
   - Local staffing (production assistants)

3. **Financial Support**
   - CDI team travel expenses (flights, lodging, per diems)
   - OR: Shared cost structure (to be negotiated)
   - Ground transportation during deployment
   - Venue costs

4. **Ghana Relationships**
   - Local production company network
   - Venue and festival connections
   - Government and media liaison
   - Regional expansion support

5. **Production Opportunities**
   - Real production work for trainees (LiveX events)
   - Crew placement pipeline for LiveX projects
   - "December in Ghana" festival participation

## KEY TERMS

### Duration
- **Initial Term:** 2 years from effective date
- **Renewal:** Automatic 1-year renewal unless 90-day written notice

### Exclusivity
- **Non-exclusive:** Both parties may engage in other partnerships
- **Right of First Refusal:** LiveX Africa has first option for CDI Ghana operations

### Equipment
- All CDI-owned equipment remains CDI property
- LiveX Africa responsible for storage, security, and insurance
- Equipment may be used for training purposes only (not commercial LiveX events without approval)

### Certificates
- Co-branded: "CDI × LiveX Africa"
- Both logos appear on all graduate certificates
- Joint announcement of graduates

### Intellectual Property
- CDI curriculum remains CDI property
- LiveX Africa may not reproduce or distribute curriculum without written consent
- Joint content (photos, videos) may be used by both parties

### Termination
- Either party may terminate with 90-day written notice
- CDI equipment to be returned or shipped within 30 days of termination
- No penalty for early termination

### Liability
- Each party responsible for own operations
- Joint liability insurance recommended for training events
- Indemnification clause (standard)

## QUARTERLY DEPLOYMENT MODEL

| Phase | CDI Responsibility | LiveX Africa Responsibility |
|-------|-------------------|----------------------------|
| **Pre-Deployment** (4-6 weeks before) | Curriculum updates, trainer coordination, sponsor communications | Trainee recruitment, venue confirmation, logistics |
| **Deployment** (2 weeks) | Training delivery, certification, show direction | Local ops, transport, catering, staffing |
| **Post-Deployment** | Certificates, crew registry, mentorship | Graduate engagement, local production work |
