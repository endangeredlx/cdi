About CDI Los Angeles

Los Angeles is the heart of the American entertainment industry. CDI LA will bring our proven training model to aspiring production professionals in Southern California, providing a pathway into the live events industry.

Our LA program will maintain the same high standards and hands-on curriculum that defines CDI, adapted for the unique opportunities and requirements of the US market.

Potential Partner: TUMO Los Angeles

We are in discussions with TUMO Los Angeles in North Hollywood as a potential venue partner. TUMO's state-of-the-art facility and commitment to creative education make them an ideal partner for CDI's expansion into Southern California.
