The Problem

Africa's live entertainment industry is booming—but the technical workforce can't keep up. International productions fly in entire crews, and local talent watches from the sidelines. The skills gap is costing the continent billions and leaving a generation without access to high-paying creative careers.

Training Model

A two-tier system designed for rapid skill development and career advancement

1
Stagehand Fundamentals

Foundation training covering essential production skills: load-in/load-out procedures, safety protocols, equipment handling, communication standards, and professional workplace conduct. All trainees complete Tier 1 before specializing.

2
Specialized Tracks

Advanced training in specific disciplines with industry-standard equipment and software. Graduates emerge job-ready with certifications.
