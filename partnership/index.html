The Opportunity

The Stage Is Set

When global artists bring their world tours to Africa, they bring everything with them—including the people who build the show.

Local technicians stand in the wings, watching foreign crews assemble stages they could build themselves, if only they had the training.

CDI exists to change that story.

Our Model

How We Build the Future

CDI operates through three interconnected programs, each designed to create sustainable change:

Learning

School-based programs and hands-on training for local technicians. University partnerships across three continents bring academic rigor to real-world skills.

Advisory

Industry professionals working alongside local crews on actual productions. Not just teaching—mentoring through live experience.

Production

Direct production support during the transition period, gradually stepping back as local teams gain confidence and capability.
